Hey! I am Fatma Saei and before I tell more about myself allow me please to ask you this .. Do you know how to make a successful trade?
As an adventurer and a passionate person I discovered the answer throughout my life. Learning and Teaching is the key of every success, and oh boy I LOVE these two. Learning is something so important that makes you and I more aware about what is around us. Thus, I love to shout-out every once in a while "What is NEW, world?" so I can learn it and then transfer it to others. By saying "transfer" I actually mean teaching. So Teaching gives me this golden chance through which I can pass what I learned, in my way, to the world. Only then can you consider yourself a successful trader.

Throughout this Learning and teaching process, I focuse on the kids and their needs. This young age needs lots of support to build up everything inside them as languistics, hobbies, values ... etc. And this what I am trying to do. I want to create a wonderful & safe world for them throughout activities, stories, games, cartoons as well as teaching.
